# AI Invoice Validator

## Problem
Manual invoice validation is slow and error-prone. Businesses lose 10-20% of revenue to billing mistakes that slip through manual review processes. Accounting teams spend 20+ hours per month checking invoices, and even then, mistakes get through.

## Solution
AI-powered invoice validation that catches 99% of errors in seconds. Our system uses machine learning trained on millions of invoices to identify billing errors, duplicates, and anomalies that human reviewers miss.

## Key Features
- **Instant validation**: Process invoices in 2-3 seconds (vs 10+ minutes manual)
- **99% accuracy**: Trained on millions of real invoices
- **Easy integration**: Works with QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks in 5 minutes
- **Saves 20+ hours/month**: Automate repetitive validation work
- **Catches duplicates**: Identify duplicate invoices across vendors
- **Flag suspicious activity**: Detect pricing anomalies and unusual patterns

## Target Customers
- Small businesses (10-100 employees) with high invoice volume
- Accounting firms managing client invoices
- SaaS companies processing subscription payments

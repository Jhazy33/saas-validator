## üéØ Opportunity

### **The Problem**

**Solo founders and indie hackers waste months building products nobody wants.**

- **90% of startups fail** because they build without validating
- **6+ months** wasted building unvalidated ideas
- **$10K-$50K** spent on development before testing market
- **No systematic way** to quickly test multiple ideas

### **The Solution**

**A web platform that validates SaaS ideas in days, not months.**

- **Generate landing pages** from any content (video, text, audio)
- **Test 10+ ideas per month** instead of 1 idea per quarter
- **Data-driven decisions** from real user behavior
- **Focus resources** on winners, kill losers quickly

## üí° Product Vision

**"The fastest way from idea to validated product"**

1. Paste a YouTube URL or describe their idea
2. Get a professional landing page in 5 minutes
3. Collect real email signups
4. See analytics and conversion rates
5. Make data-driven decisions (build, pivot, or kill)
